The Christian Life

What is Christianity?

The Christianity is a religion based on the faith in the teachings of Jesus Christ. People believes that God sent Jesus to live as a human being in order to save Humanity from the consequences of its sins.

What do Christians believe?

Christians believe there is only one God, but He is revealed in three different forms : God the Father, God the Son and The Holy Spirit.

What is the Christian Holy Book?

The name of the eternal book that will save thousands of lives is BIBLE.
